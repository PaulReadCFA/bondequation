Equation 6 expresses the price of a coupon bond as:

PV Coupon bond = PMT r × [ 1 − 1 ( 1 + r ) T ] + FV ( 1 + r ) T
Present value of the coupon bond equals the coupon payment divided by the yield, multiplied by one minus one over one plus the yield to the power T, plus the face value divided by one plus the yield to the power T.
